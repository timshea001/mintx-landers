LiteLift® is a revolutionary trash bag featuring dual-handle technology - the first and only of its kind in the world.

- **World's first** trash bag with dual-handle design

- Can be lifted solo or with assistance from another person
- Ergonomic design reduces strain and protects your back

### Strength & Durability
- **True 3 MIL Thickness** for uncompromised strength
- Industrial-level seals prevent leakage
- Handles the biggest loads with confidence

### Capacity
- **42-gallon capacity** for heavy-duty applications
- Contractor-grade performance
